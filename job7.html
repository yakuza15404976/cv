Responsibilities will include, but not limited to:
  • Identify procurement method based on factors such as price, available resources, and urgency of the requirement
  • Review and edit purchase requests to ensure that item or service is adequately described and complies with relevant regulations
  • Helped supervisors and subordinates to identify and resolve problems
  • Analyzes quotations and determines the most suitable vendors
  • Ordering store supplies when the supplies are low
  • Uses computerized systems to process a purchase order, research vendors, and produce reports as required
  • Confers with department personnel concerning the availability
  • Receives, analyzes, and edits requisitions for the purchase of materials, supplies, and equipment
  • Helped plan and coordinate events and/or meetings
  • Information storage and Management and Website design
